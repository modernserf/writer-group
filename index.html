What is Write to Speak?

Write to Speak is a workshop for people interested in speaking at tech meetups and conferences. Whether you're putting the finishing touches on a talk or you have only the smallest inkling of an idea, we want to help you bring your ideas to life.

At Write to Speak, you'll get feedback from experienced organizers, speakers and community members to guide your ideas into proposals, and your proposals into talks.

Code of Conduct

We take safety and inclusivity seriously at Write to Speak, and all atendees are expected to follow the JSConf Code of Conduct.
